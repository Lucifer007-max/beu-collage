Our Mission

This involves a range of activities aimed at enhancing employability and ensuring successful placements.

Our Vision

The Vision of Training and Placement (TNP) Cell in Bihar Engineering University, Patna is to empower students with the resources and guidance necessary to achieve their career aspirations, whether through securing employment, pursuing higher education, or engaging in entrepreneurial endeavours. This vision involves the premier facilitator of industry-academia partnerships and ensure 100% placement for our eligible students, facilitating connections between students and potential employers, providing career counselling, and offering training programs to enhance student’s skills and employability. Ultimately, the TNP Cell aims to bridge the gap between academia and industry; ensuring students are well-prepared for successful careers.

Students from various engineering and architecture disciplines

Students are enrolled for B.Tech (4 year course) with 34 specializations, B.Arch (5 year course), for M.Tech (2 year course) with 20 specializations and MBA Students of 38 State Government Engineering Colleges, 1 Central Government Engineering College & 19 Private Engineering Colleges affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna in the state of Bihar. Students get admission through the JEE (Mains) examination for B. Tech Course and the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination (BCECE) for B. Tech/ B.Arch/ M.Tech Course. This provides our students with the right aptitude, which is very critical. These students are put through a rigorous, high intensity programs, which includes Industrial training, partnering with faculty in research, and participation in relevant forums and conferences, thereby making them truly industry ready. The assessment mechanisms followed are rigorous and require students to not just put in their best curriculum-based effort, but also applied learning gained through industry exposure.
