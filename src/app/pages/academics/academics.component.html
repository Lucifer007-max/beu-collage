    National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)

    The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is an annual ranking system introduced by India's Ministry of Education in 2015 to evaluate universities, colleges, and institutions across various categories such as Overall, Universities, Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Medical, Law, and more. NIRF aims to enhance transparency, promote competition, and improve the quality of higher education in India.
