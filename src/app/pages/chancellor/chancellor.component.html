Prof. Suresh Kant Verma

Vice- Chancellor

Prof Suresh Kant Verma is currently serving as Vice Chancellor, Bihar Engineering University, Patna, Bihar. Prior to this, he was Deputy Director, National Institute of Technology, Patna. He holds B.Sc (Engg.), M.Sc (Engg.) and Ph.D (Engg.) from BIT, Sindri, Ranchi University, Ranchi. His Specialization is Heat Power Engineering. His area of research includes Solar Energy, Stirling Cycle Engine and Computational Fluid Dynamics. He has also contributed in the development of NIT Patna by taking responsibility at key positions like Head of the Department (Mechanical Engineering), Dean (Student Welfare), Dean (Academic) and Chairman (ACoFAR). He is Fellow of the Institution of Engineers (India). He has also the membership of Professional Societies like, Indian Society of Mechanical Engineers, Indian Society for Technical Education, Solar Energy Society of India and American Society of Mechanical Engineers. He has published the research papers in the National and International Journals and also has presented papers in the International and National conferences. He has guided M.Sc (Engg.) / M.Tech and Ph.D research scholars.
